Powerful features designed with love and privacy in mind

Stick. Tap. Remember.

Buy cheap NFC stickers online. Stick them on anything: a wedding album, grandma's jewelry box, your kid's first drawing. When you tap, your iPhone instantly opens the photos and story you saved.

AI Writes Your Story

Add photos, and Apple Intelligence automatically writes a beautiful story, summary, or timeline. All processing happens on your iPhone - nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Your Memories Stay Yours

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Optional iCloud sync only between YOUR devices.

Organize in Capsules

Create memory capsules for each occasion: "Summer 2025", "Mom's Birthday", "Our Trip to Paris". Add as many photos as you want.

Share the Magic

Give a tagged gift to someone. When they tap it with their iPhone, they'll see the memories you attached. Perfect for meaningful presents.
